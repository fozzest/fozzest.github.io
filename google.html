Overview

Bain is a world-renowned consultancy, with a rising digital presence under the name of ADAPT (Advanced Digital and Product Team). As the first ever intern at ADAPT, I took on a range of projects, including the production of a ‚Äòproduct story‚Äô of one of our clients, which we will call Cend.

Tools

Primer Prompt

Option 2

Design an experience for students to discover orientation events and craft a visual system to accommodate different types of events: sports, music, visual arts, social groups, and volunteering events. Provide high-fidelity mocks for searching, browsing, and viewing the details for these different events.

Define The Problem Research

In order to share this story, Cend had to be redesigned for client confidentiality. Through this process, I gained valuable insights into how brand identity can be visually addressed in the digital product, both with broader strokes such as colour and font, but also less tangible qualities such as nuanced motion and grid systems.

Researching Pain Points

In creating the sanitiesd version of the product, I devised a new hypothetical name and logo that evoked the qualities that ADAPT had lifted in pronounced during the case work. This formed the basis for the rest of my visual exploration, which included an extensive dark-themed iteration. We eventually settled on the fluid but sophisticated scheme you see today.
